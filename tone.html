Tone

Ursula's tone shifts continuously throughout the song. Where her tone feels manipulative, oddly alluring, and darkly comedic. Ursula's actions appear playful, sinister, and mischievous. The tone shifts reflect the growing control and manipulation.



"My dear, sweet child...", "Now I've got her, boys, the boss is on a roll", and "Boss is on a roll." The tone shifts from calm, and nurturing tone to triumphant as Ursula gains the upper hand. This intense shift reflects Ariel's vulnerability and Ursula's supremacy, highlighting how she successfully and strategically used persuasion to achieve her desire.
