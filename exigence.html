Exigence

The story arises from Ariel’s desperation for a human life and Ursula’s ambition for power. Ariel's desire gives Ursula the perfect opportunity to strike a deal that benefits her - the chance to gain control over King Triton by using his daughter.



"And don't underestimate the importance of body language!" Ursula uses her performance as well as Ariel's eagerness to establish a sense of pressure. Her body language and tone enhances her persuasiveness.

External exigence: Ariel's desire | Internal exigence: Ursula's determination.
