Context

The events unravel in the underwater kingdom of Atlantica, during which Ariel seems to have lost hope of ever being able to be with Prince Eric in the 'human releam,' making her vulnerable. In which she visits Ursula's lair as a last resort, Ursula steps in and presents herself as the only solution Ariel has if she wants to achieve her desire.



"Life's full of tough choices, ain't it?" Ursula's convienent timing and sarcastic yet composed tone exploit Ariel's emotional state. Her casual language makes her approach feel almost natural and relatable, therefore increasing the likelihood of Ariel agreeing to the contract.
